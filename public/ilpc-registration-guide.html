How to Register for
ILPC 2026

Fresh Oil for a New Season · June 5–7, Akute, Nigeria

✦ Registration is FREE ✦

We are so glad you're joining us. Follow these simple steps below and you'll be registered in less than 2 minutes. We can't wait to see you there!

1

Open the Registration Page

On your phone or computer, open your browser and go to:

ilpc.heartbeatofgod.ca
2

Click "Register Free"

Scroll down the page until you see the Register Free button — it's bold and easy to find. Tap or click it to open the registration form.

3

Fill in Your Details

Enter your full name, email address, and phone number. Please double-check your email — it must be correct, because your confirmation and QR code will be sent there.

4

Submit Your Registration

Click the Submit or Register Now button. You'll see a confirmation message on screen — that means you're in! Your seat is reserved. 🎉

📬

Check Your Spam Folder!

Your confirmation email may land in your Spam or Junk folder. If you don't see it in your inbox within a few minutes, please check there. Once you find it, mark it as "Not Spam" so you never miss a message from us.

📱

Bring Your QR Code to the Venue

Inside your confirmation email is a personal QR code. This is your entry pass. Print it out or save it on your phone — our team will scan it at the entrance and you will receive your conference materials right there. Please do not forget it!

The conference holds on June 5–7, 2026 at
200 Akute Rd, opp. Royal Prince Supermarket, Akute, Nigeria.
