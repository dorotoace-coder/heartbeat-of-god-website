How to Register for
ILPC 2026

Fresh Oil for a New Season  ·  June 5–7  ·  Akute, Nigeria

✦   Registration is Absolutely FREE   ✦

We are so glad you're joining us. Follow these simple steps below and you'll be registered in less than 2 minutes. We cannot wait to see you there!

1

Open the Registration Page

On your phone or computer, open your browser and visit:

ilpc.heartbeatofgod.ca
2

Click "Register Free"

Scroll down the page until you see the bold Register Free button. Tap or click it to open the registration form.

3

Fill in Your Details

Enter your full name, email address, and phone number. Please double-check your email — it must be correct, because your confirmation and QR code will be sent there.

4

Submit & You're In!

Click Register Now. You'll see a confirmation message — your seat is reserved! 🎉

📬

Check Your Spam Folder!

Your confirmation email may land in your Spam or Junk folder. If you don't see it within a few minutes, check there and mark it "Not Spam" so you never miss a word from us.

📱

Bring Your QR Code to the Venue

Your confirmation email contains a personal QR code — this is your entry pass. Print it or save it on your phone. Our team will scan it at the entrance and hand you your conference materials right there. Please do not forget it!
